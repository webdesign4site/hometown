ABOUT US

“Home is the starting place of love, hope and dreams and a place where our story begins.”

Over the past 10 years, HomeTown has been bringing the latest designs & fashion to Indian homes. HomeTown offers the widest and best in class range in furniture, home furnishings & decor, modular kitchens, home improvement and more. Part of the Future Group, HomeTown brings an enjoyable and hassle-free homemaking experience to all its valuable customers with varying lifestyles and preferences.


We promise to facilitate our customers with a unique and personalized shopping experience. Our commitment to quality and timeless designs has helped us evolve over the years and it indeed fills us with pride to be the first choice of many.


With a great spread that appeals to the globe-trotting, trendy yet very much Indian homemaker, HomeTown is known to attract an array of lifestyle seeking customers. Being recognized as India’s biggest store in homemaking, renovation and decor, our products are exclusively designed while keeping durability and comfort at priority.
